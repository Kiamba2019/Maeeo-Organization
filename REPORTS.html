FAQ

What is MAEEO?

MUNDU AGRICULTURAL ENVIRONMENTAL AND EDUCATIONAL ORGANIZATION

What is it all about?

MAEEO works with grass root community groups, and especially women and youth groups, through design, implementation and monitoring of projects. The thematic areas of the projects are in sustainable ecological agriculture and produce marketing; environmental conservation and awareness creation {tree nurseries establishment and tree planting, renewable energy, water and sanitation, solid waste management and nature based enterprises}; community empowerment and capacity building with emphasis on women and youth; and mainstreaming of cross cutting issues (HIV/AIDS, reproductive health, human rights, good governance and gender). Consultancy work is also provided as source of income and for sustainability. It is not discriminative and works with all people regardless of age, religion, political affiliation, gender or colour.

Can I partner with MAEEO?

MAEEO works and focuses on grass root community groups irrespective of their gender composition though it has a bias on women and youth groups mainly. We forge partnership in implementation of programmes and projects in the above areas of focus.

MAEEO involves the communities in decision-making process in the entire programme and project cycle phases through their elected representatives. Participatory approach in needs assessment and prioritisation is embraced in any activities initiation. Community capacity building is emphasised for activities ownership and sustainability.

MAEEO partners with individuals, community based organizations, non-governmental organizations, local authorities, government departments and any other willing development partners. However, the communities and their institutions are the key partners. Participation in stakeholder’s forums is done to address issues of networking and collaboration.
