OUR APPROACH

MAEEO works and focuses on grass root community groups irrespective of their gender composition though it has a bias on women and youth groups mainly. We forge partnership in implementation of programmes and projects in the above areas of focus.

MAEEO involves the communities in decision-making process in all the programme and project cycle phases through their elected representatives. Participatory approach in needs assessment and prioritisation is embraced in any activities initiation. Community capacity building is emphasised for activities ownership and sustainability.  

MAEEO partners with individuals, community based organizations, non-governmental organizations, local authorities, government departments and any other willing development partners. However, the communities and their institutions are the key partners. Participation in stakeholder’s forums is done to address issues of networking and collaboration.
